About Us
Pizza King La Crosse first opened in December 1975, offering exclusively take and bake pizzas. Over the years, however, the menu has been expanded greatly, and now includes: fresh baked pizzas, submarine sandwiches, gyros, salads, wings, party platters, and more. Pizza King has become a favorite in the La Crosse area, especially due to its proximity to UW La Crosse. People all over the tri-state area enjoy our pizzas hot and fresh out of their home ovens. Offering top quality, fresh made foods at a reasonable price has always been the standard at Pizza King. Our Owner, Terry Mach, started making pizzas back in March 1976 at the young age of 16! Terry's brother Steve, and sons Allan, Ray, and Ken also work currently and help keep the place running smoothly. Our business known for being run by principles of family, great value, high quality, and tradition.
We love what we do, and we hope you do too!
